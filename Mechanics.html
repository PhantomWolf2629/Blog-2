What Is a Mechanic within a game?

Game Mechanics are the definition of how player can interact within the rules of the games. In a more detail description it's the formal properties of the game for example the games goals, players action and strategies, and game states.

What strategies that will help you?

As a strategies I'll start with hero roles. As you know theirs the Damage Dealers, Tanks, and Supports. I suggest focusing on one of these roles but for a casual player this doesn't matter. As the Tanks focus on protecting your team instead of running in their to do some damage even though it's tempting. For the Damage Dealers stay behind your Tanks to stay alive longer to deal more damage. The Supports works either behind the Damage Dealer or the Tank.
